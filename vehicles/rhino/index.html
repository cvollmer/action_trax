Tracking Vehicles : Yamaha Rhino

Yamaha Rhino

The Rhino works well in all types of conditions - sand, mud, snow, water, etc. This is a great choice for your on and off road needs.

  • Designed for fast and easy rigging.
  • This unit can accomodate all camera systems and is outfitted with a 1.5 inch speed rail system.
  • The Rhino can carry up to six persons and can attain speeds up to 45 mph.
  • Compact sized vehicle: 4’6”W x 10’ 6”L
  • Equipped with front, rear & overhead mounting plates.
