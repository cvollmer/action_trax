Tracking Vehicles : Yamaha 1200 V-Max

Yamaha 1200 V-Max

An excellent and versatile tool to use with Steadicam, Libra Remote heads, hard mounts with fluid heads, hand held, still photography and more.

  • Right or left air suspended camera platforms. The 72"L x 36" platforms are outfitted with an adjustable 1.5" speedrail system.
  • Fully padded working areas for the safety and comfort of the camera operators.
  • Adjustable camera plates that adapt to all Grip and Camera packages.
